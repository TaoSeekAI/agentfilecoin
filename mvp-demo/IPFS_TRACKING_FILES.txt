• parse_error   - Content retrieved but invalid

SEVERITY LEVELS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Critical - Metadata unavailable (NFT non-functional)
• High     - Primary asset permanently lost
• Medium   - Temporary gateway/timeout issues
• Low      - Minor retryable errors
